PRIVACY POLICY!

What information do we collect?

We collect information from you when you register on our site, specifically information already publicly available on your Facebook or Google+ account.

As you use The Reckoner, your activity on the site is associated with your account, including your voting, posting, and favoriting activity.

Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on this site. Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to users based on their visit to your sites and other sites on the Internet. You can opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.

What do we use your information for?

Any of the information we collect from you may be used in one of the following ways:

  • To personalize your Reckoner experience! This includes associating your activity on The Reckoner (votes, posted questions, favorited items) with your user account.
  • To administer any Reckoner-related contests, media features, or other site features.
  • To improve The Reckoner! We love feedback, and we're always trying to make the website better based on the information and feedback you provide!

How do we protect your information?

The content itself is stored within The Reckoner's servers, which are protected through network firewalls and protocol encryption.

If you wish to have any personally associated information removed from the Reckoner, contact us via the information at the bottom of the form, and we'll remove it within 24 hours.

Do we use cookies?

Yep. We use cookies to understand and save your preferences for future visits! These are mostly so you don't have to log back into the site every time you come back.

Cookies are small files that a site or its service provider transfers to your computers hard drive through your Web browser (if you allow) that enables the sites or service providers systems to recognize your browser and capture and remember certain information. You probably already have a quadrillion of these things from nearly every website you've ever visited.

Do we disclose any information to outside parties?

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties your personally identifiable information. This does not include trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or servicing you, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential. We may also release your information when we believe release is appropriate to comply with the law, enforce our site policies, or protect ours or others rights, property, or safety. However, non-personally identifiable visitor information may be provided to other parties for marketing, advertising, or other uses.

California Online Privacy Protection Act Compliance

Because we value your privacy we have taken the necessary precautions to be in compliance with the California Online Privacy Protection Act. We therefore will not distribute your personal information to outside parties without your consent.

As part of the California Online Privacy Protection Act, all users of our site may make any changes to their information at any time by logging into their control panel and going to their profile page, as accessible via the link in the profile box in the site title bar.

Childrens Online Privacy Protection Act Compliance

We are in compliance with the requirements of COPPA (Childrens Online Privacy Protection Act), we do not collect any information from anyone under 13 years of age. Our website, products and services are all directed to people who are at least 13 years old or older.

Online Privacy Policy Only

This online privacy policy applies only to information collected through our website and not to information collected offline.

Your Consent

By using our site, you consent to our privacy policy.
