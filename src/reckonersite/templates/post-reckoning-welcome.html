POST A RECKONING!

Want to use The Reckoner to settle a decision?
Awesome! Welcome aboard!

We're always happy to help, but Reckonbot can be a harsh mistress, and not every question makes it through. Here are a few helpful tips to ensure your Reckoning gets onto the site and helps you settle things once and for all!

Have Two Clearly Opposed Options

Your fellow Reckonauts are going to need a clear sense of what they're voting between. This or that. Yankees versus Red Sox. Alien versus Predator. DTMFA versus *don't* DTMFA.

Larry The Cable Guy-style 'Women: Amirite or Amirite' questions make Reckonbot emit a combination of ozone and fresh tears, so don't do that.

Be Even-Handed

We all love to have our preconceptions confirmed -- it's an awesome feeling! At the same time, we can't put our thumb on the scale. Take the Dragnet approach to your Reckoning -- 'just the facts, ma'am'. Sergeant Joe Friday will think you're the coolest, and so will Reckonbot!

Use Your Best English

Reckonbot was an English Literature major at Robot Polytechnic University (B.A. 1976), and was voted 'Most Likely to Make a Pedantic Observation About the Use of Subjunctive Versus Indicative Verb Tense in Subordinate Clauses'. It's gotten better since then, but it's best to stay on its good side.

Do Not Spam

This one isn't for you -- you're the best and we love you! This is for that other guy who's thinking about posting spam or an empty self link under the guise of a 'Reckoning'. We're totally going to ban that guy.
